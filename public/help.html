Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Booking

  • Browse our listings, select your preferred room, choose your dates, and follow the on-screen instructions to complete your booking. You'll receive a confirmation email with all the details.

  • Yes, you can modify your booking through your account dashboard. Changes are subject to availability and may incur additional charges depending on the property's policy.

Cancellations

  • Cancellation policies vary by property. You can find the specific policy for your booking in the confirmation email or your account dashboard. Generally, cancellations made 48+ hours before check-in receive full refunds.

  • Refunds typically process within 5-7 business days, depending on your bank or payment method. You'll receive an email confirmation once processed.

Payments

  • We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), UPI payments, and popular digital wallets like Paytm and PhonePe.

  • Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and never store your full payment details on our servers. All transactions are processed through PCI-compliant payment gateways.
